02 · THE FIX

Why Existing Apps Aren't Enough

Timetable apps show you when. Overpass shows you everything — including what no other app covers.

THE GAP OTHER APPS LEAVE

Timetables.
Nothing more.
Nothing else.

Other transit apps are great at departure boards — but stop there. They don't tell you if the M7 is gridlocked, whether your station has a working lift, or that your Opal card will tap out at Redfern.

  • ❌ No road conditions — transit and roads live in separate apps
  • ❌ No real accessibility tools — just a font size toggle at best
  • ❌ No Opal visibility — balance, taps, and top-up reminders missing
  • ❌ No haptic stop alerts — you're watching the screen or missing your stop
  • ❌ NightRide treated as an afterthought, not a first-class service
WHAT ONLY OVERPASS DOES

Transit.
Roads. Opal.
Accessibility.

Overpass layers live TfNSW transit data with motorway conditions, Opal card management, and the deepest set of accessibility features in any NSW transport app.

  • ✅ M1–M8 road conditions alongside train departure boards
  • ✅ 10 accessibility features — dyslexia mode, haptics, step-free routing
  • ✅ Opal balance, tap history & low-balance warnings in one place
  • ✅ Haptic buzz one stop before your destination — screen off, DND on
  • ✅ NightRide as a full first-class network, not a footnote
